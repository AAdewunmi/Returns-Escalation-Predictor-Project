Operational proof points

Core system behaviors designed to stay consistent across future surfaces.

Operational clarity

Cases, notes, events, and documents are modelled separately so later surfaces can show the same truth without template-only workflow logic.

Deterministic setup

Demo users, roles, and seeded cases are created through a repeatable command, which keeps tests and walkthroughs predictable from the start.

Shared pagination contract

Queue and portal list behaviour will inherit one page-size contract with stable ordering and filter-preserving links instead of per-page reinvention.

Current sprint scope

Sprint 1 ships the public shell, domain foundations, deterministic data setup, and shared UI primitives. Auth flows, queues, and portal workflows land in later sprints.
